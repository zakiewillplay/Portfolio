About

Building for the web.
Obsessing over detail.
Shipping things that work.

I'm a web developer based in India, focused on websites, web apps, and full e-commerce experiences built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Next.js. I pay close attention to interaction design and layer in detail-driven GSAP animation, but I never let motion get in the way of usability. Every project starts with the same questions: how fast does it load, how well does it rank, and how easily can someone actually use it.

That approach has taken me from education platforms and coaching institutes to business sites, online stores, and custom digital products for clients across industries. I care about performance, SEO, and clean, usable interfaces just as much as I care about how something looks — because a beautiful site that's slow or confusing isn't actually doing its job. Whether I'm working solo or alongside a team, my goal is the same: turn an idea into a product that's fast, findable, and genuinely enjoyable to use.
